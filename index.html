What We Do

Four questions.
One platform.

Where

Location Intelligence

Know where your assets, shipments, and resources are — in real time, at any scale, across any geography.

How They Move

Movement Tracking

Trace every step of an asset's journey — routes, handoffs, velocity, and anomalies — with full audit trails and real-time alerts.

Who They Belong To

Ownership Clarity

Maintain an unambiguous chain of custody and provenance. Always know who holds what — and prove it to anyone who asks.

Whether They're Trusted

Trust Verification

Automated trust scoring, compliance checks, and anomaly detection so every decision is grounded in verified truth.

How It Works

Secure software,
built to scale with you.

1

Ingest

Connect existing data sources — IoT sensors, ERP systems, logistics APIs, and custom feeds — through our unified ingestion layer. No rip-and-replace required.

2

Analyze

Our engine processes movement, ownership, and trust signals in real time, enriching data with context, flags, and verified provenance across your entire network.

3

Act

Surface insights through dashboards, alerts, and APIs your teams already use. Trigger workflows automatically when something moves, changes hands, or fails a trust check.

Everything you need
to answer the hard questions.
